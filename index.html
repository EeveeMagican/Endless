RP Story Highlight

The Following are the sections of RP that are going on with our main Home, which is the Conan Exiles game on {info on server here}

  • Our Story Part 1

    Having met in World of Warcraft (Azeroth) and joined the same Endless journey...

    We have seen when our Endless clan was at it's peak as well as at it's lowest points. We remember the mountains we came from, the guilds that rose high... many a journey there was before World of Warcraft, yet only those who have been with us the longest remember them.

    We have carried the spark and memories with us everywhere we have placed the Endless flag. While we have not forgotten our roots, we have traveled far. We often find ourselves reminiscing on the old days as a consequence, and it brings comforting memories.

  • Our Story Part 2

    Weathering the Storms and Waters of Life

    Life hit hard, in many ways shapes and forms across the board for all of our Endless members. Despite this, and even in long gaps of silence, we felt each others presence and could not be more grateful for them.

    Holding to what we cherish most in our lives, our loved ones, our honor, our valor... we have braved many storms. And still, here we are - Endless.

  • Our Story Part 3

    Arrival to Conan Exiles

    Dead or alive, alive or dead, us at Endless realize there's not much if any difference between the two. As we travel we are who we are - and we will as we are as well.

    As we band together for the 13th time since our leader Genkaku found his calling in life, we are reminded that though we have gone through so much as to make us feel like sand on the line of a beach only overlooking a horizon and never knowing if we'll be built again - there is still left in us the desire to go on. So go on we shall, living as though we never got hampered in the first place. Healing from our wounds, and becoming stronger as we progress in this lesson of life.
