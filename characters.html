Characters Highlight

The following is a character profile of those active on Conan Exiles, {info on server here}

  • Genkaku

    Shrouded in mystery, and he prefers to leave it that way.

    Just like others who appeared on Conan Exiles, he was saved from the cross and spends his time saving others. He stays close to those known as the Endless, but otherwise keeps to himself.

  • Eevee Magican

    A mage who mainly talks with Genkaku, prefers peace and knowledge but is keen with a bow whenn necessary.

    Eevee was originally born as a fox demon in outer space near a black hole with her family. She was sent to her home planet to get to know their ways. However as time passed and although she made friends that she wouldn't want to part with (Sabin, Kalu, Yahiko, Genkaku, Dessi, and plenty more) she was soon called back home and noone had heard from her since. However she herself had awoken here, on Conan Exiles. She was saved by Genkaku, and since has spent her days trying to make a peaceful world in a less than peaceful place. Anyone who has spent time on Conan knows why this would be challenging. After all, no-one but the erased has ever left this place, and not being able to return to where ones attachments are can make some feral in this place. If anyone asks how she came to arrive here, she struggles to remember and often ends up with a bit of a headache after attempting to recall it. To avoid this discomfort, she typically says she doesn't feel like discussing it.

    Eevee's likes and dislikes are pretty straightforward. She enjoys anything that brings knowledge without harming others, and she enjoys healing people. She spends her spare time comparing the accuracy and strength of arrows - as in this world you can never be too ready. You can also find her enjoying the scenery when supplies do not need to be gathered.
